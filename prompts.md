# AI Prompt Library – Practical Use Cases

A collection of real prompts used in training, coaching, and business workflows.

---

## 1. Career Clarity Prompt
Act as a career coach. Based on my experience, skills, and interests, help me identify:
- My ideal niche
- High-income opportunities
- Skills I should develop next

---

## 2. LinkedIn Content Creation Prompt
Act as a LinkedIn content strategist. Create a high-engagement post on [topic] with:
- Strong hook
- Story or insight
- Clear call-to-action

---

## 3. Webinar Script Prompt
Act as a sales strategist. Create a webinar script for [audience] that includes:
- Hook
- Problem agitation
- Solution framework
- Offer pitch
- Call-to-action

---

## 4. Course Creation Prompt
Act as an instructional designer. Help me create a 3-module course on [topic] including:
- Module titles
- Key lessons
- Activities and assignments

---

## 5. Sales Copy Prompt
Act as a copywriter. Write a persuasive sales page for [product/service] including:
- Headline
- Benefits
- Objection handling
- CTA

---

## 6. AI Automation Workflow Prompt
Act as an automation expert. Design a workflow using AI + tools like Zapier/Pabbly to:
- Capture leads
- Send follow-ups
- Nurture prospects automatically

---

## 7. Personal Branding Prompt
Act as a personal branding expert. Help me define:
- My positioning
- Target audience
- Content strategy
Based on my experience in [domain]

---

## 8. Problem-Solution Mapping Prompt
Act as a business strategist. Identify:
- Top 5 problems faced by [target audience]
- Possible solutions I can offer
- Monetization ideas

---

## 9. Coaching Session Prompt
Act as a mentor. Ask me powerful questions to help me:
- Gain clarity
- Overcome self-doubt
- Take action on my goals

---

## 10. Productivity Optimization Prompt
Act as a productivity coach. Help me:
- Plan my daily schedule
- Prioritize high-impact tasks
- Eliminate distractions
Based on my goals

---

## Purpose
These prompts are actively used in:
- AI training sessions
- Coaching programs
- Business systems

Focus: Practical execution, not theory.
